04. Knowledge Check — End of Week 1

Complete these by the end of Week 1. There are no trick questions — we are looking for clarity of thinking and your own words, not memorized definitions.

  1. What is a token in the context of an LLM, and why does context window size matter?
  2. What is the difference between an LLM and an AI agent?
  3. Explain RAG in one paragraph. Why is it useful for working with internal company data?
  4. What is a blockchain and why is every transaction traceable?
  5. What is a crypto mixer and why does it complicate forensic investigations?
  6. What is a cross-chain bridge and why do investigators need to track them?
  7. You are given a suspicious wallet address. Walk through the steps you would take to investigate it using both blockchain data and AI tools.
  8. What makes a good AI prompt? Write a prompt you would use to summarize a suspicious transaction for a law enforcement report.
  9. What is one thing you learned this week that surprised you or changed how you think about AI or crypto?

Haven256 is a crypto asset custody and liquidation firm operating as a division of HashWorks Industries, specializing in the secure handling of seized and forfeited digital assets on behalf of government agencies and law enforcement.

The Role

You'll own a real internal project from day one — building AI and blockchain intelligence tools that solve operational problems. Your code ships. By week 12 you'll hand off a working prototype to the team.

Most importantly, you're independent, intellectually curious, and ready to build something real.
